About

This app lets you browse through a collection of short notes on cases for the final MBBS examination. Content is oriented towards Sri Lankan medical colleges. The content is under active ongoing development. Your contribution to making this app a success is always appreciated.

 

Contribute

Join the Academic contributors WhatsApp group to learn more about how to contribute. Direct your bug reports and suggestion to the issues section of the repository.
