About

This app lets you browse through a collection of short notes on cases for the final MBBS examination. Consise set of notes for quick reference. Content is oriented towards undergraduates studying in Sri Lankan medical colleges. The content is under active ongoing development. Your contribution to making this app a success is always appreciated.

Contribute

The app is still under development. Academic content in the app is crowdsourced. You can contribute towards the content by joining the WhatsApp group. The latest list of topics along with their status can be found in the GitHub repository.

Report issues

Please report the bugs at the issues section of the repository. Inaccuracies in the notes are also issues. Follow this guide to report an issue.
