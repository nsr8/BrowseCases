ABOUT

This app lets you browse through a collection of short notes on cases for the final MBBS examination. Content is oriented towards the final MBBS examination in Sri Lankan medical colleges. The content is under active ongoing development. Your contribution to make this app a success is always appreciated.

 

Contribute
Please email the developer at mnzuhair@gmail.com if you are interested in contributing to this app. Please send in your short notes and cases lists to publish in this app! You can contribute towards development of the app by pulling the GitHub repository. Please do not email bug reports. Post all bug reports to the Issues section of the GitHub repository at https://github.com/nsr8/BrowseCases/issues.
